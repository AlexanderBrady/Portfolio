Project Overview:

Pollen Pursuit is an innovative augmented reality (AR) game designed to blend educational content with interactive technology, set within the beautiful Camellia House at Wollaton Hall. The game leverages mobile AR technology to simulate an engaging learning experience about the critical role of bees in pollination.

Gameplay Highlights:

Players navigate through the botanical garden, using their mobile devices to find and scan flower-coded markers. Each successful scan scores points and unlocks educational snippets about the plant and its ecological importance. The straightforward yet compelling gameplay mechanics are designed to appeal to a broad audience, enhancing both educational outreach and user engagement.
